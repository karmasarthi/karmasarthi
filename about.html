About Karma Sarthi

Your trusted partner in navigating the journey from student to professional

Our Story

Karma Sarthi was born from a simple observation: too many students graduate without clear direction, leading to career dissatisfaction and wasted potential. Our founder, after mentoring dozens of students through successful career transitions, recognized the need for accessible, personalized guidance.

The name "Karma Sarthi" combines the Sanskrit words for "action" and "guide" - reflecting our mission to be your companion in career action.

Today, we've helped hundreds of students discover meaningful career paths and build the confidence to pursue them.

Our Mission & Approach

We believe every student deserves clarity, direction, and support in their career journey

Our Mission

  • Empower students with clarity about their career
  • Provide actionable roadmaps for professional growth
  • Build confidence through personalized mentorship

Our Approach

  • Student-first perspective in all our guidance
  • Evidence-based career assessment and planning
  • Long-term mentorship focused on sustainable growth
